Introduction

The UAD-01 is an unassembled educational drone platform by Scientech. It enables students and professionals to learn drone mechanics, assembly, electronics, and autonomous programming hands-on.

Key Features

  • Modular design for 25+ experiments
  • Compatible with Mission Planner for autonomous flight
  • Ideal for robotics, aeronautics, and embedded systems learners

Hardware Components

  • Quadcopter frame, landing gear, BLDC motors
  • 4 ESCs (40A), STM32 Flight Controller
  • GPS, LiDAR, FPV Camera + TX
  • 6+ channel RC Transmitter/Receiver
  • 3300mAh LiPo Battery

Flight Control & Working

  • Throttle: Vertical movement
  • Pitch/Roll/Yaw: Directional tilt and rotation
  • Lift, Thrust, Drag: Forces during flight

Assembly & Safety

  • Secure motor wiring and propeller placement
  • Mount GPS, ESC, LiDAR properly
  • Test electronics before propeller attachment
  • Double-check battery and alignment

Software & Setup

  • Use Mission Planner to flash firmware
  • Calibrate sensors: Accelerometer, Compass, ESC, RC
  • Configure flight modes: Stabilize, Loiter, RTL

Advanced Features

  • LiDAR: Accurate altitude hold
  • FPV Camera: Real-time video via mobile app
  • Waypoint missions with GPS
  • PID tuning for flight behavior

Applications & Learning

  • Aerial photography & monitoring
  • Research & environmental surveys
  • Embedded systems & control theory
